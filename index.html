About

My mother is Syilx, my father is Gitxsan. I myself was born in Vancouver, raised in the Okanagan, and spent a few years down in the states. Back in Canada and excited to explore more of BC. All contents are local to the Okanagan, all photos shot by me :). Thanks for visiting.
